Future Projects

Published: Wed 01 January 2042
By Eduard

In Future Projects.

tags: OverviewAnnotation

Every now and then I get inspired to take on a new big project. Usually there is the promise of some big social or environmental impact and other times it just seems cool. I will never have the capacity to execute all of them, so I've put them out here in the hope that someone else might stumble across them and take up the baton. Alternatively if there is a very conscientious investor out there that wants to give me a big pile of cash to make one of them work, I would certainly be amenable to the idea. Note that some of my "past projects" would also fall into this category. Iv'e usually been good at getting from a new idea to POC that demonstrates something cool and new. Getting from there to a viable business is where I have consistently fallen short. Sad face.
